Progressive Web Apps (PWAs) are a hot topic in the world of e-commerce. But what exactly are they and do they make sense for your store?

There are several benefits to implementing a PWA for your store:

Improved performance: PWAs can load faster and have a more responsive design than traditional websites, leading to a better user experience.

Increased conversions: PWAs have been shown to increase conversions, as they provide a more seamless and intuitive shopping experience for users. According to a study by Google, PWAs can lead to an average conversion rate increase of 20%.

If you want to improve the performance and user experience of your store, and you don't need access to advanced device features, a PWA may be a good choice.
